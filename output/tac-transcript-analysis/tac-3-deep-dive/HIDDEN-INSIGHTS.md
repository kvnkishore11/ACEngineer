**Only in Video**:

**Hidden Insight**: The goal isn't job security through complexity, but through orchestration capability. You become irreplaceable by being the one who can make agents productive, not by writing code others can't understand.

**Video Quote**:

**Hidden Insight**: The author sees a hierarchy - those who just generate code with AI vs those who orchestrate entire engineering workflows. TAC-3 is about ascending to the latter.

### 3. The "Phase Two" Mindset
**Video Context**:
> "This compressed, focused version of the software development lifecycle is built for phase two of the generative AI age."

**Not in Code**: The author frames everything in terms of "phases" of AI evolution:

### 4. The "Betting on Permanence" Strategy
**Video Wisdom**:

**Applied Insight**: The SDLC is positioned as the unchanging foundation worth betting on, regardless of which AI tools win.

- Complex systems: Templates for specific bug classes

**Future Vision**: The author is preparing students for a world where agents run autonomously in the cloud, not in your local terminal.
